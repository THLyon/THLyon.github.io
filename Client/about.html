About Me

I am

Tanner

Most of my day-to-day, until my contracted position recently ended, was spent working as a software engineer at Codesmith, commonly contributing to the code base with a tech stack that focused on React, Redux, NodeJS and postgreSQL. My most recent work has been building an Open Source Product, SolidStructure, where my team and I engineered a developer tool for the frontend library SolidJS. The product featured dependency graphs and component trees to display the relationships between components and SolidJS signals in a chrome extension dev tool. Additionally, I continue to work on products that utilize React Hooks, Typescript, NodeJS, TDD and both relational and non-relational databases. Check out my work at Github, and my photography at Instagram.
